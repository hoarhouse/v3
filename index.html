Flagship Initiative

THE BUDAPEST APPEAL

The Budapest Appeal for Digital Ceasefire

The Budapest Appeal for a Cyber Ceasefire and Digital Peace was conceived following discussions regarding a potential high-level summit in Hungary. Budapest — where Domus Communis Foundation maintains an active presence — emerged as a natural symbolic reference point, reflecting DCF's mission to foster dialogue across the Global North, South, East, and West. The initiative frames digital de-escalation and peace-building within a Sovereign AI and data sovereignty model, affirming that technological restraint, human dignity, and national agency must advance together. The Budapest Appeal continues as an open initiative, welcoming support from major AI actors, global civil society leaders, academia, Nobel Laureates, faith leaders, and institutions committed to peace and the common good.
