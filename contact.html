Frequently Asked Questions

What is DCF Hungary?
DCF Hungary is part of a global nonprofit constellation dedicated to ensuring AI and emerging cyber technology serves humanity. We bridge technologists, academics, faith leaders, and institutions to advance value-based technology development.
Do I need to be Catholic to get involved?
No. While our work is inspired by Catholic Social Teaching, we welcome and serve people of all faiths and backgrounds. Our focus is on shared human values and value-based technology.
How can my organization partner with DCF?
We collaborate with universities, tech companies, NGOs, and faith organizations. Contact us using the form above to discuss partnership opportunities.
How are donations used?
Donations directly fund our eight initiatives: Peace, Technology, Academia, Education, Mentorship, Research, Regulatory, and Theology/Philosophy programs advancing sovereign AI.
Where is DCF located?
DCF operates as a global constellation with presence in Washington D.C. (USA), Budapest (Hungary), and Accra (Ghana).
