Our Product Portfolio

Nine specialized solutions addressing critical European market needs

Health Companion

Your Personal Health Assistant

A mobile app that stores all your health information in one place and helps you understand it. Chat with an AI assistant about your symptoms, medications, and health questions. You control who sees your data—share with doctors when YOU decide, not when they ask.

Learn more →

ResearchPay

Get Paid When Researchers Use Your Health Data

Pharmaceutical companies and universities need health data for research. ResearchPay lets you contribute your anonymized health data to studies and get paid €150-300 per year for it. Researchers see patterns and statistics—never your actual identity or records.

Learn more →

LabVault

All Your Lab Results in One Place

Blood tests from your doctor, results from specialists, screenings from the hospital—LabVault collects them all into one unified view. See trends over time, get plain-English explanations of what your numbers mean, and share specific results with any healthcare provider.

Learn more →

Argus

Cybersecurity That Learns From Everyone's Attacks

When one company gets attacked, Argus helps ALL companies learn from it—without anyone sharing their sensitive data. Our AI learns attack patterns across hundreds of organizations so your security team can stop threats before they hit you.

Learn more →

ALETHEIA

Verify Professional Credentials Across Borders

Is that doctor licensed? Is that engineer's degree real? ALETHEIA checks professional credentials across EU countries in minutes instead of months. Licensing bodies keep their data private while employers get instant verification.

Learn more →

PROMETHEUS

Accurate Carbon Emissions Data From Your Supply Chain

Companies must report their suppliers' carbon emissions, but suppliers won't share production data (it reveals trade secrets). PROMETHEUS calculates emissions inside supplier systems and returns only the carbon numbers—accurate data without exposing anyone's secrets.

Learn more →

NEMESIS

Detect Procurement Fraud Across Europe

€2 trillion in EU public contracts. Billions lost to fraud. NEMESIS spots suspicious patterns—bid rigging, shell companies, corrupt networks—across 27 countries without any nation sharing sensitive procurement data with another.

Learn more →

HYPERION

Identity Management for Light-Based Computers

Photonic computers use light instead of electricity—they're faster and more efficient. HYPERION verifies which photonic computing nodes are legitimate, who owns them, and what they're allowed to do. Like a passport system for next-generation AI hardware.

Learn more →

ARCHON

Identity Management for AI Agents

Companies are deploying AI agents that act autonomously—accessing data, making decisions, executing transactions. ARCHON gives each AI agent a verified identity, controls what it can do, and links it to a responsible human. Like HR for your AI workforce.

Learn more →
