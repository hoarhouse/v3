## KEY INSIGHTS FOR PROMETHEUS POSITIONING
-----------------------------------------

### Market Opportunity
- Carbon accounting software: $11.86-18.52B market growing at 23.9-38.5% CAGR
- ESG software: Additional $1.08-1.92B market
- Europe leading adoption with 35.3% revenue share
- CSRD affecting 50,000 companies (though may be reduced)

### Pricing Landscape
- Free tiers emerging (Persefoni Pro)
- Enterprise solutions $37K-264K (Watershed)
- Salesforce at $48K-210K
- Gap for mid-market federated solution

### Critical Pain Points
- Scope 3 = 70-80% of emissions but worst data quality
- Only 40% of companies tracking supply chain emissions
- Spend-based estimates inaccurate but most common
- Primary data collection faces massive barriers

### Regulatory Momentum
- CSRD implementation 2024-2025 (Europe)
- CBAM requiring carbon tracking for imports
- SEC dropped Scope 3 but California mandating it
- Clear trend toward mandatory Scope 3 reporting globally
