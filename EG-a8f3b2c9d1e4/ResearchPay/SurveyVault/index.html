Frequently Asked Questions

How is my anonymity guaranteed?

We use zero-knowledge cryptographic proofs that mathematically guarantee your identity cannot be revealed. Your responses are verified without exposing who you are. Even SurveyVault employees cannot connect survey responses to individual users - it's technically impossible with our architecture.

How quickly do I get paid?

Payment is instant upon survey completion. Money appears in your dashboard immediately and can be withdrawn to your bank account within minutes. No minimum thresholds, no waiting periods, no processing delays. Your time is valuable and we respect that.

What happens to my health data?

Your health data never leaves your encrypted sovereign vault. When researchers want to query your data, they send questions to your vault, which processes them locally and returns only anonymized statistical responses. Your raw health data remains under your complete control forever.

Who conducts these surveys?

All surveys come from verified research institutions including universities, medical centers, and approved research organizations. We vet every researcher and study to ensure legitimacy. Examples include Harvard Medical School, University of Oxford, Mayo Clinic, and European health research councils.

Can I opt out of studies later?

Absolutely. You can revoke consent from any study at any time. Your data contributions are immediately removed from research datasets. You have complete retroactive control - if you change your mind about participating in a study, your data is permanently excluded.
