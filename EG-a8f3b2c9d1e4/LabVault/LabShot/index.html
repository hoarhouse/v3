(Ref: <100 optimal)

‚Üí
LabShot Translation

Your 'bad' cholesterol (LDL) is 142. Optimal is below 100. You're in the 'borderline high' range. Lifestyle changes often help. Consider discussing with your doctor.

Example 2: Kidney Function

Medical Report

Creatinine: 1.2 mg/dL
(Ref: 0.7-1.3)

‚Üí
LabShot Translation

This measures kidney function. Your level (1.2) is in the normal range. Your kidneys are filtering waste from your blood properly.

Example 3: Diabetes Screening

Medical Report

HbA1c: 5.9%
(Ref: <5.7% normal)

‚Üí
LabShot Translation

This shows your 3-month average blood sugar. At 5.9%, you're in the 'prediabetic' range. Not diabetes yet, but worth watching. Talk to your doctor about prevention.
