LabVault

All your lab results. One app. Finally organized.

LabVault is your personal app for managing all your lab results in one place. Blood tests from your doctor, panels from the hospital, results from Quest or LabCorp—everything organized in a single timeline on your phone. No more logging into five different portals or digging through old emails.

See all your results at a glance. Tap any test to understand what the numbers mean. Track how your cholesterol, blood sugar, or any marker changes over time with simple charts. When you visit a new doctor, share exactly the results they need with one tap—no more carrying paper copies or trying to remember passwords.

Unlike hospital portals that only show their own results, LabVault pulls everything together from every provider you've ever used. Your data is stored in your own encrypted vault—not on hospital servers or in the cloud. You own it, you control it, and you decide who sees it. Built on E-Group's European health data infrastructure, LabVault gives you the privacy and portability that fragmented hospital systems can't.

How It Works

Three simple steps to unified lab history with complete control

1

Aggregate Your Lab History

Upload PDFs from any lab (Quest, LabCorp, hospital, private labs). Our OCR + AI extraction reads any format automatically. Or connect directly via FHIR integration with major lab systems.

  • MyD Wallet authenticates to multiple lab portals
  • InnoHealth DataLake stores unified history in YOUR vault
  • Location: YOUR choice (EU-hosted, on-premise)
2

AI Analysis & Insights

COIOS translates medical terminology to plain English while FedX runs trend analysis on your encrypted data.

  • Trend detection: Identifies patterns over months/years
  • FedX runs analysis ON your encrypted data
  • Contextual insights: Factors in age, family history, medications
  • Alerts: "This value outside normal range - discuss with doctor"
3

Share with Your Doctors

Doctor visit coming up? Share relevant results with granular control through IOX.

  • Granular control: "Share only cholesterol panels, not kidney function"
  • Time-limited: Access expires after appointment automatically
  • Federated: Doctor queries YOUR vault (data never leaves)
  • Audit trail: "Dr. Anderson accessed cholesterol: Nov 15, 2:34pm"
