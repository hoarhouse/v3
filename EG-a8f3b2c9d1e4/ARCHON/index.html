ARCHON

Identity & Access Management for AI Agents

ARCHON is an identity management system for AI agents—the autonomous software programs that companies are deploying to automate tasks, make decisions, and access sensitive data.

Just like employees need ID badges and access permissions, AI agents need verified identities and controlled access. ARCHON provides this by:

  • Issuing digital credentials to each AI agent so you know exactly which agents are operating in your systems
  • Controlling what each agent can do with real-time permission enforcement
  • Linking every agent to a responsible human so there's always accountability
  • Logging every action for compliance and audit purposes

Think of ARCHON as 'HR for your AI workforce'—onboarding agents with verified identities, defining what they're allowed to do, and maintaining records of everything they do.

Built on E-Group's eIDAS 2.0 certified MyD Wallet, ARCHON is the EU's first sovereign identity infrastructure for AI agents. While US solutions like Strivacity and Okta bolt governance onto cloud-centric platforms, ARCHON is built from the ground up for European data sovereignty. Federated architecture means your agents don't phone home to American servers. GDPR, EU AI Act, DORA, and NIS2 compliance is native, not an afterthought.
