Scope 3 Reporting Is Fundamentally Broken

The Visibility Gap

75-84% of your carbon footprint comes from your supply chain. But suppliers won't share production data because it reveals competitive secrets - process efficiency, energy sources, material costs.

Spend-Based Estimates Are Fiction

Current methods use industry averages that can be off by 200-300%. A supplier using 100% renewable energy looks identical to one burning coal. Auditors increasingly reject these estimates.

Manual Surveys Don't Scale

Supplier questionnaires achieve 20-30% response rates with unreliable, self-reported data. A typical enterprise has 5,000+ tier-1 suppliers. The math doesn't work.

Regulatory Deadline Is Here

CSRD requires 50,000+ EU companies to report Scope 3 emissions starting 2025. Non-compliance means fines up to ‚Ç¨75,000 and potential criminal liability for executives.
