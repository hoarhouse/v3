About DCF

Building ethical technology for the common good of humankind

Our Mission

Domus Communis Foundation is a global constellation dedicated to ensuring artificial intelligence and cyber technology serves humanity rather than the reverse. We build a 'cyber commons' — a globally networked collaborative space where ethical technology development operates through shared resources, collective governance, and mutual benefit.

Guided by Catholic Social Teaching principles while serving people of all faiths, we bridge major AI and technology actors, global civil society leaders, academia, Nobel Laureates, faith leaders, and institutions to shape a future where AI serves humanity, peace, and human dignity.

Our Guiding Principles

Inspired by Catholic Social Teaching

Human Dignity

Every person possesses inherent worth; technology must serve and protect this dignity

Common Good

Technology should benefit all of society, not just the privileged few

Subsidiarity

Decisions should be made at the most local level possible

Solidarity

Technology must foster interconnection and mutual support

Preferential Option for the Poor

Technology should bridge gaps, not widen them

Care for Creation

Sustainable innovation preserving the earth for future generations

While inspired by Catholic principles, DCF welcomes and serves people of all faiths and backgrounds.

An Interfaith Commitment

In 2026, we honor the 800th anniversary of Saint Francis of Assisi's historic dialogue with Sultan Malik al-Kamil — a model of interfaith encounter that guides our work bridging faith traditions in service of ethical technology and human dignity.

We also support the Rome Call for AI Ethics, led by the Pontifical Academy for Life, promoting Transparency, Inclusion, Responsibility, Impartiality, Reliability, and Security in AI development.

The Global Constellation

DCF is designed as a constellation structure connecting the Global North, South, East, and West with nonprofit foundation entities located across the globe.

Washington D.C., USA

Global North leadership and policy influence

Budapest, Hungary

Technical hub, European connections, R&D

Accra, Ghana

Global South bridge, African engagement

Why Budapest?

Budapest serves as a natural center point connecting global regions, leveraging Hungary's rich scientific legacy — including computing pioneer John von Neumann — and its position bridging Eastern and Western Europe.
