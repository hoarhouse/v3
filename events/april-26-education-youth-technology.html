Education, Youth & Technological Responsibility

A dialogue on digital transformation, formation, and the educational mission of faith communities

📍 Parish of Santa Maria delle Grazie — Casal Boccone, Rome  |  4:00 PM – 6:00 PM

Register to Attend Share This Event

About This Event

The first meeting addresses digital transformation starting from the formation of individuals, with particular attention to young people. Artificial intelligence and digital technologies are profoundly changing the way we learn, work, and relate to one another. In this context, the educational mission of the Church and local communities becomes increasingly important.

The Salesian tradition, together with the perspective of integral ecology, offers a valuable framework for accompanying younger generations in a responsible and conscious way.
