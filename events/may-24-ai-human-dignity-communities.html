About This Event / L’Incontro

The second meeting represents the natural development of the journey begun on May 7. Attention shifts to the concrete orientation of technological development and the construction of relationships among communities. The Domus Communis Foundation presents its model as a possible space for collaboration between territories, institutions, and international realities.

Il secondo incontro rappresenta lo sviluppo naturale del percorso avviato il 7 maggio. L’attenzione si sposta sull’orientamento concreto dello sviluppo tecnologico e sulla costruzione di relazioni tra comunità.

About the Domus Communis Foundation

The Domus Communis Foundation is an international constellation of nonprofits dedicated to the research, development, and promotion of human-centric, ethical, safe, and secure cyber and AI technologies for the benefit of humankind, the preservation of peace, and the advancement of the common good.
