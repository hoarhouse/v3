About This Event

The second meeting focuses on guiding technological development and building relationships among communities. Current transformations call for renewed efforts to ensure that technology serves the human person, the freedom of communities, and the construction of peace.

The Domus Communis Foundation will present its model of technological development and its international network as a platform for collaboration among communities, institutions, and academia.

About the Domus Communis Foundation

The Domus Communis Foundation is an international constellation of nonprofits dedicated to the research, development, and promotion of human-centric, ethical, safe, and secure cyber and AI technologies for the benefit of humankind, the preservation of peace, and the advancement of the common good.
