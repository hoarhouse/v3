Our Initiatives

Eight strategic programs advancing ethical technology for humanity

DCF advances its mission through eight interconnected initiatives, each addressing critical aspects of ethical technology development. Together, they form a comprehensive approach to ensuring AI and cyber technology serve the common good.

PEACE

Digital Peace Process for Global Stability

Programs for a Digital Peace Process to end cyber hostilities, cyber disinformation, and autonomous AI weapon systems in conjunction with peace efforts to stop military hostilities for sustainable integral peace.

Key Programs:

  • Digital Peace Initiative bringing together government leaders, technologists, faith leaders, and academia
  • Planning and advocacy for Cyber Ceasefire
  • Ending autonomous AI weaponry and cyber disinformation

TECHNOLOGY

Solutions for Ethical Digital Sovereignty

Solutions for ethical digital sovereignty and sovereign AI for the common good of humankind and for serving vulnerable populations worldwide to eliminate the digital divide.

Key Programs:

  • Enabling and providing data sovereign platforms
  • Sovereign AI and agentic AI with values controlled by users
  • Goal: fully enabled pod available and within reach of everyone

ACADEMIA

Connecting Global Academic Excellence

Bringing together academicians and academia from around the world for research and aggregating library resources on ethics and technology for advancement of cyber and AI to benefit humankind.

Key Programs:

  • Developing an academic commons for academicians and academic institutions
  • Sharing information and collaboration for ethical, value-based, human-centered cyber and AI technologies

EDUCATION

Programs for Cyber Safety and Ethical AI

Programs in cybersecurity, cyber safety, and human-centered AI curricula for secular, faith-based, and Catholic schools; students; teachers; parents; and caregivers.

Key Programs:

  • Interactive experiential learning platform and simulations
  • Cyber safety, cyberbullying, and leadership training
  • Certification programs for ethical AI technologies
  • Curriculum for Catholic and faith-based schools

MENTORSHIP

Guiding the Next Generation of Ethical Developers

Mentorship and technology programs to guide young and emerging developers and programmers with ethical, value-based, human-centered principles throughout their innovation journey.

Key Programs:

  • Providing a global mentoring network
  • Live training and e-learning platforms
  • Connecting young developers with capital and opportunities

RESEARCH

Accelerating Human-Centric AI Innovation

Supporting and aggregating pioneering scientific research to guide and accelerate human-centric, ethical, safe cyber and AI technologies to benefit humanity.

Key Programs:

  • Developing research commons for global collaboration
  • Connecting researchers with commercial applications
  • Sharing research and resources through the technology community

REGULATORY

Building Effective Standards and Regulations

Provide connectivity to all communities with the regulatory agencies to develop effective regulations and standards.

Key Programs:

  • Creating a regulatory commons for global connection
  • Best practice sharing between regulators
  • Connecting regulators with technologists, researchers, faith leaders, and academics
  • Identifying standards for regulatory guidance and human protection

THEOLOGY/PHILOSOPHY

Interfaith Commons for Value-Based Technology

Provide an interfaith commons and sharing of values for the continued development and advancement of ethical, value-based, human-centered cyber and AI technologies bringing faith and science to work closely together.

Key Programs:

  • Creating the interfaith commons
  • Providing value-based integration into developing cyber and AI technologies
  • Maintaining cultural and societal values worldwide
