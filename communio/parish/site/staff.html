Meet the Team

Our Parish Staff

The dedicated team serving St. Mary's Parish community with faith, compassion, and excellence.

📖
Pastor
Fr. Michael O'Brien

Father O'Brien has served St. Mary's since 2015. Ordained in 1998, he holds a doctorate in Sacred Theology from the Gregorian University in Rome and is passionate about building authentic community in the heart of New York City. When not celebrating Mass or in the confessional, he enjoys reading philosophy, visiting parishioners in need, and cheering on the Yankees.

pastor@stmarysnyc.org
⛪
Associate Pastor
Fr. David Martinez

Father Martinez joined St. Mary's in 2019. He oversees youth ministry, coordinates our Spanish-language Masses, and leads our outreach to the local Hispanic community.

associate@stmarysnyc.org
🕊️
Deacon
Deacon Robert Chen

Deacon Chen was ordained in 2021 and serves our community with particular focus on marriage preparation, hospital visits, and assisting with baptisms and funerals.

deacon@stmarysnyc.org
📋
Parish Manager
Maria Santos

Maria coordinates the day-to-day operations of our parish, manages our volunteers, and ensures everything runs smoothly. She has been with St. Mary's for over twelve years.

manager@stmarysnyc.org
🎵
Music Director
James Thompson

James leads our parish choir and coordinates music for all liturgical celebrations. He holds a Master's in Sacred Music and has been serving Catholic communities for fifteen years.

music@stmarysnyc.org

Faith Formation Director

formation@stmarysnyc.org
