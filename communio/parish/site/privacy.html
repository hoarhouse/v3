Privacy Policy

St. Mary's Parish — New York

Last updated: March 2025

Who we are

St. Mary's Parish is a Roman Catholic parish located at 123 Rivington Street, New York, NY 10002. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you share with us through our website.

Information we collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or submit a form:

  • Your name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Information you provide in contact, registration, or sacrament request forms
  • Newsletter subscription preferences
  • General usage data through anonymised analytics (no personal identifiers)

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and requests
  • Process sacrament and event registrations
  • Send our weekly parish bulletin and announcements (if you subscribe)
  • Improve our website and communications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our site.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To make a request, please contact us at info@stmarysnyc.org or call (212) 555-0123.

Third-party services

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages and uses Google Fonts and Google Maps. These services may collect anonymised usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please get in touch or write to us at 123 Rivington Street, New York, NY 10002.
