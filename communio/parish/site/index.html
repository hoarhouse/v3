Our Story

More than a parish. A family.

For over 135 years, St. Mary's has been a beacon of faith and community in New York's Lower East Side. We are a diverse Catholic community that welcomes all people to experience the love of Christ through worship, fellowship, and service.

Whether you're visiting New York, new to the neighborhood, or seeking a spiritual home, we invite you to join our parish family and grow in faith together.
