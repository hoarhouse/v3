Sacrament of Initiation

Baptism

Baptism is the first and most fundamental sacrament, the gateway to life in the Spirit and to the other sacraments. We celebrate Baptisms for infants and children throughout the year, and for adults through the RCIA process.

1
Submit this request formWe will contact you within 2 business days to discuss next steps.
2
Attend a Baptism preparation sessionRequired for parents and godparents of infants. Sessions run monthly.
3
Schedule the dateBaptisms are typically celebrated on Sunday afternoons after the 11:00 AM Mass.

Sacrament of Matrimony

Wedding at St. Mary's

Marriage is a sacred covenant between a man and a woman, witnessed by the Church. St. Mary's is a beautiful setting for your wedding, and our team will accompany you every step of the way. Please contact us at least six months in advance.

1
Submit your enquiryAt least 6 months before your preferred date. We will check availability and reach out.
2
Meet with Fr. O'BrienAn initial meeting to discuss your faith background and marriage preparation requirements.
3
Complete Pre-CanaMarriage preparation programme — required by the Archdiocese of New York.
4
Confirm your date and plan the liturgyWork with our music director and liturgy coordinator to plan your ceremony.
Note on availability Saturdays book quickly. We recommend reaching out as early as possible. Contact weddings@stmarysnyc.org

Sacrament of Initiation

Confirmation

Confirmation completes the grace of Baptism and seals the recipient with the Gift of the Holy Spirit. Our Confirmation programme runs annually for young people in Year 10 and above, and adults who were not confirmed at the time of their First Communion.

1
Register in SeptemberOur Confirmation programme begins each September. Early registration is encouraged.
2
Two-year preparationCandidates attend formation sessions, retreats, and service requirements over two years.
3
Receive the SacramentConfirmation is typically celebrated in Spring with the Archbishop or his delegate.
Questions about Confirmation? Contact Sofia Morales, Director of Faith Formation: formation@stmarysnyc.org
Confirmation Registration
Register your interest and our Faith Formation team will be in touch with programme details.

Registration opens each September. We'll confirm receipt and send programme details.

Sacrament of the Eucharist

First Holy Communion

First Holy Communion is one of the most joyful milestones in a Catholic child's life. Our preparation programme ensures children and their families are well supported as they receive the Body of Christ for the first time.

1
Register in SeptemberChildren are typically in Year 2 (age 7–8). Registration opens each September.
2
Weekly faith formation classesChildren attend classes on Sunday mornings through the school year.
3
First ReconciliationReceived in February, preparing the child spiritually for the Eucharist.
4
First Communion MassCelebrated in May with family and the parish community.
Questions? Contact Sofia Morales: formation@stmarysnyc.org
First Communion Registration
