The Problem

Training AI on satellite data
is slow, expensive, and incomplete.

Earth observation AI systems require enormous volumes of labelled satellite imagery to train effectively. The problem: real-world satellite data is expensive to acquire, slow to process, geographically uneven, and critically sparse for rare events — exactly the scenarios where AI systems need to perform most reliably.

Rare weather events, conflict zones, disaster scenarios, unusual atmospheric conditions — the real world does not produce training data on demand. And waiting for it means AI models that are undertrained on the cases that matter most.

GENESIS solves this by generating it. Photorealistic synthetic satellite imagery, produced at scale, for any scenario, at any location, under any conditions. Training data that reflects the world as it could be — not just as it has been photographed.

Capabilities

What GENESIS delivers.
On demand.

Synthetic Dataset Generation
Produces vast volumes of photorealistic synthetic satellite imagery on demand. Any geography, any resolution, any atmospheric condition. Training datasets that would take years to acquire from real satellites — generated in hours.
Rare Event Simulation
Generates imagery of scenarios that real satellite archives cannot provide at sufficient volume — natural disasters, conflict damage, extreme weather events. The edge cases where AI performance matters most and real training data is scarcest.
Archive Gap-Filling
Fills gaps in existing satellite archives — missing time periods, cloud-covered regions, underrepresented geographies. Provides continuity in datasets that real-world acquisition cannot guarantee.
Predictive Scenario Modelling
Generates imagery of future scenarios — urban development, deforestation projections, climate change impacts. Training AI systems to detect changes before they are fully visible in real imagery.
Anomaly Detection Training
Creates synthetic anomalies injected into real imagery for anomaly detection AI training. Enables validation of detection models against ground truth that cannot be reliably sourced from real satellite archives.
End-to-End EO AI Strategy
GENESIS is part of E-Group's broader Earth Observation AI strategy alongside ProofX and SpadaX — a complete stack from synthetic training data to real-world verification and anomaly detection.
Applications

Where synthetic satellite imagery
changes the equation.

The organisations that need GENESIS are those building AI systems that depend on satellite imagery — and who cannot wait years for real-world data to accumulate at the scale and diversity their models require.

Defence and intelligence — training reconnaissance AI on scenarios that cannot be safely or legally captured in real imagery. Climate and environmental — modelling scenarios that do not yet exist in satellite archives. Infrastructure and urban planning — simulating future states of built environments. Disaster response — training AI on damage scenarios before disasters occur.

In each case, the value proposition is the same: AI systems that perform reliably on the scenarios that matter most, trained on data that real-world acquisition cannot provide.
