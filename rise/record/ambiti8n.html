Ambiti8n —
Europe's federated AI integration pilot

Live · Rotterdam DemonstratedIPCEI-CIS WS38 Partners4 Countries11 ComponentsMarch 2026

What It Is

Ambiti8n is the flagship integration pilot of IPCEI-CIS Workstream 3 — a working federated AI platform demonstrated live at the 8ra Annual Summit in Rotterdam on 19–20 March 2026. Eight organisations, four countries, eleven interoperable components, one live application. Built in four months.

It is not a prototype. It is not a proposal. It is a complete application stack — operational, cross-border, sovereign, and aligned with European law — showing that federated AI infrastructure can be built and deployed across EU member states without compromising data sovereignty.

The Live Demonstration

On 19–20 March 2026, Ambiti8n was demonstrated live at the 8ra Annual Summit in Rotterdam, in front of approximately 500 European stakeholders — partners, policymakers, and European Commission representatives. The summit was organised by the Netherlands Enterprise Agency (RVO) under the Dutch Ministry of Economic Affairs.

The demonstration ran a clinical AI application — sleep apnea detection — federated across hospital sites in Italy and Poland, with inference at the hospital edge and sovereign EU cloud deployment. Patient data never moved. Gradient updates travelled. The model converged round by round across the federated hospital infrastructure.

"The complexity is real. The simplicity is the achievement."

— Antal Kuthy, E-Group CEO, Workstream 3 Lead

The Problem

Sensitive data cannot move. AI needs data to work.

Across European healthcare, manufacturing, finance, and automotive sectors, organisations hold high-value datasets that AI could transform. The obstacle is always the same: GDPR, data residency law, and hospital policy prevent the centralisation that conventional AI training requires.

Ambiti8n resolves this at infrastructure level. The AI model travels to the data. Training happens locally. Insights are shared. Raw data stays where it is.

What Was Built

Eleven interoperable components, developed by eight specialist organisations, integrated into one working platform:

  • Hospital edge sites in Italy and Poland
  • Federated AI Factory hosted at Gdańsk University of Technology
  • FedX federated learning (E-Group) — CNN-LSTM trained locally, gradients aggregated centrally
  • KServe edge inference (E-Group) — model deployed at the hospital
  • Villanova 2B sovereign language model on Sherlock inference platform (CloudFerro)
  • AI Model Catalog (Engineering)
  • Lab8ra Kubernetes platform, MLflow tracking, anonymisation (Gdańsk TU)
  • App packaging via Helm charts and Kafka monitoring (Reply)
  • Clinician-facing app, data pipeline, CNN-LSTM model (Result)
  • Service orchestration and cross-provider networking (TIM)
  • openEHR + OMOP Common Data Model as the mandatory semantic layer

Two AI Engines, One Application

Ambiti8n runs two AI models in one application — each chosen for what it does best, deployed where it works best.

The CNN-LSTM model (Result) detects sleep apnea from patient SpO₂ and heart rate signals, sampled at one-second frequency across one-minute sleep windows. Trained on real-world data plus the HuGCDN2014-OXI dataset. Trained federally via FedX — data stays in the hospital, gradients aggregate at the Gdańsk University of Technology AI Factory. Deployed at the hospital edge via E-Group's KServe inference. Low latency. Local audit log. No cloud round-trip.

The Villanova 2B LLM (Tiscali / Villanova) converts the CNN output into a structured clinical diagnostic report, in all major EU languages — addressing the non-English language gap that standard LLMs underserve. Too large for hospital-edge deployment, it runs on Sherlock, CloudFerro's sovereign EU inference platform in Poland, as a project-specific instance rather than a public API. Engineering's AI Model Catalog connects LLM training output to the Sherlock deployment pipeline.

E-Group's Role

E-Group leads IPCEI-CIS Workstream 3, leads its AI Cluster, and drives integration across the Ambiti8n consortium — stitching components from eight partners into a working system. E-Group's FedX platform provides the federated learning engine; edge inference is delivered via KServe on FedX.

E-Group also contributed directly to the IPCEI-CIS Reference Architecture (ICRA v2.0) — the strategic framework that defines the cloud-edge continuum Ambiti8n operates on. Workstream 3's mission is to develop the advanced, modular processing services for the Multi-Provider Cloud-Edge Continuum; Ambiti8n is the first live proof that those services work together as a coherent whole.

The Consortium

Eight specialist organisations across four countries. Each owns a distinct, non-overlapping layer.

  • CloudFerro (Poland) — Sovereign EU cloud, Sherlock LLM inference platform
  • E-Group (Hungary) — Federated learning, edge inference, FedX, integration lead
  • Engineering (Italy) — AI Model Catalog
  • Gdańsk University of Technology (Poland) — Lab8ra platform, MLflow, anonymisation
  • Reply (Italy) — App packaging, monitoring
  • Result (Slovenia) — Clinician-facing app, data pipeline, CNN-LSTM model
  • TIM (Italy) — Service orchestration, cross-provider networking
  • Tiscali / Villanova (Italy) — Villanova 2B sovereign multilingual LLM, Altea EU AI Act compliance platform

Compliance and Governance

Tiscali's Altea platform delivers EU AI Act compliance oversight for Ambiti8n. The Altea Compliance Report dated 5 March 2026 (Code 2635, verified by Benedetta Giovanola and Emanuele Frontoni) confirms privacy-by-design, strong data quality under openEHR and OMOP, and coherent role-based access control. The report issues an intermediate maturity verdict — technically coherent, governance-aware, valid as a WS3 integration demonstrator. Audit granularity remains under active development, consistent with the system's production readiness trajectory.

What Comes Next

The Ambiti8n architecture is sector-agnostic. The healthcare demonstration is the first live proof; the same federated AI infrastructure is designed to generalise into manufacturing, finance, automotive, energy, and public sector deployments.

FedX — E-Group's principal exploitation platform and the federated learning engine behind Ambiti8n — already operates across five verticals: healthcare, automotive, cloud-edge services, federated language models, and agentic AI coordination.

At Rotterdam, FedEU.ai — the commercial platform that emerges from this research and integration work — was introduced to European Commission representatives, Member State delegations, and the broader cloud ecosystem through positioning in the CISERO exploitation roadmap. IPCEI-CIS targets a ready-to-scale validation verdict by the end of 2026.
