What It Is

Corporate banking.
Built to last.

CORPEX is E-Group's corporate internet banking platform — a proven, production-grade front-end and infrastructure layer for financial institutions serving corporate clients. It is not a prototype. Not a pilot. A system that has been running in financial institutions across Central Europe for over twenty years.

Built on E-Group's thirty years of FinTech expertise — clearing, settlement, payment systems, regulatory compliance — CORPEX brings that depth of institutional knowledge into a deployable, maintainable banking platform.

Financial institutions do not replace core banking infrastructure lightly. The fact that CORPEX has been in continuous production for two decades is the most credible endorsement there is.

Capabilities

What CORPEX delivers.
In production.

Corporate Internet Banking
Full-featured corporate banking front-end. Account management, payment initiation, transaction history, multi-user corporate access with role-based permissions. Built for the complexity of corporate treasury operations.
Payment Infrastructure
Payment processing and routing infrastructure built on E-Group's clearing and settlement expertise. Domestic and cross-border payment support with full audit trail and regulatory reporting.
Regulatory Compliance
Built to comply with EU financial regulation — PSD2, AML, KYC, and reporting requirements. Designed to adapt as regulation evolves, not to be replaced every time it does.
Security Architecture
Enterprise-grade security built on E-Group's identity and authentication expertise. Multi-factor authentication, session management, and audit logging — built by the team that built Hungary's national digital identity system.
Integration Layer
Core banking integration layer connecting CORPEX to existing financial institution infrastructure. Designed for the complex, legacy-heavy environment that real financial institutions actually operate in.
White-Label Ready
Configurable for the branding and workflow requirements of individual financial institutions. Built as a platform, not a fixed product — adaptable to the specific needs of each deployment.
