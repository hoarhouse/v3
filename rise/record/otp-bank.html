What It Is

Payment gateway and settlement infrastructure built for OTP Bank — Hungary's largest commercial bank. A multi-year partnership delivering mission-critical financial transaction processing systems for both retail and corporate banking operations.

What E-Group Delivered

The partnership covered the core financial plumbing that banks depend on: payment gateway infrastructure, clearing and settlement systems, and transaction processing at national scale. Real-time card transaction processing between banks and merchants, built in highly regulated environments.

In 2011, the partnership expanded. E-Group built the payment gateway between OTP and Union Pay — one of the world's largest card payment networks. The gateway handles ISO8583 protocol conversion, Thales HSM-secured PIN processing, and operates under a 99.99% reliability SLA with 24/7 support. The 400,000+ transactions per day at peak run through this gateway. See the full Union Pay record for complete technical detail.

This was part of the broader wave of financial infrastructure modernisation across Hungary in the 2000s — the era when E-Group established itself as a trusted builder of banking systems.

Context

OTP Bank is Hungary's largest and most systemically important commercial bank. Building payment infrastructure for this institution required the same level of trust, security, and reliability that characterises all of E-Group's critical infrastructure work — from MAVIR's electricity grid to KAÜ's national identity system.
