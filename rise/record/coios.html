What it is
A platform, not a product.
The foundation SIs build on.

COIOS gives system integrators a complete, sovereign agentic AI platform they can license, customise, and white-label for their own enterprise customers. It provides the infrastructure, compliance framework, and agentic AI capabilities — while the assembly of specific solutions remains the integrator's domain.

Building this kind of platform from scratch costs system integrators €5–10M+ and takes years. COIOS removes that barrier entirely. An SI can license COIOS, assemble a sovereign AI offering for their customers, and take it to market — passing procurement reviews that hyperscaler-only approaches fail on data residency and EU AI Act compliance grounds.

For regulated enterprises that cannot use centralised AI — because their data cannot leave the building, the country, or the jurisdiction — COIOS delivers complete end-to-end agentic AI solutions with full data sovereignty built in.

Nine pillars
What COIOS delivers.
All nine. Not a selection.
01
Data Sovereignty
Complete organisational control over data assets with zero unauthorised access. Data physically remains within designated jurisdictions. All movements are fully monitored, logged, and traceable.
02
Privacy-Preserving AI
Advanced AI capabilities — reasoning, planning, multi-agent coordination — without sharing sensitive data with third parties. Federated learning enables continuous self-improvement while raw data stays decentralised.
03
Agentic AI
Intelligent decision-making with multi-step reasoning and autonomous business process support. Agents understand complex queries, create dynamic execution plans, interact with third-party systems via MCP, and complete tasks autonomously — with configurable human oversight.
04
Cost Reduction
Significant operational savings versus centralised solutions. Organisations use existing server capacity across their networks rather than investing in expensive GPU centres or monthly cloud licensing fees.
05
Regulatory Compliance
Designed from the ground up for GDPR, HIPAA, EU AI Act, and similar regulations. Data localisation, comprehensive audit trails, and compliance-by-design architecture reduce legal exposure and compliance team workload.
06
Collaborative Intelligence
Multi-country, multi-continent collaboration on sensitive datasets previously off-limits for centralised AI. Agents share insights and analytics across sites without centralising sensitive data.
07
Self-Optimisation
Systems autonomously adapt to local conditions. With federated learning support, the platform continuously learns and improves — providing faster, more accurate decision-making at edge nodes over time.
08
Scalability
Distributed architecture enables efficient scaling across networks without proportional infrastructure increases. Edge processing reduces data transmission and network load while supporting growing computational needs.
09
European Ecosystem
Built by European engineers with deep EU regulatory knowledge. Supports European digital sovereignty and independence from foreign AI dependencies — aligned with EU strategic autonomy and IPCEI-CIS objectives.

Why E-Group builds this

Now pointed at agentic AI.
