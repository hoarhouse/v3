Founders

Two people. One company. 1993.

AK
Antal Kuthy
Co-founder & CEO
M.Sc. Telecommunication Engineering and Computer Science, BME. ProScientia Gold Medal — national science competition, 1st place. IBM R&D Heidelberg. NTT Research Tokyo. FCO Chevening Scholar in financial economics, UCL London. Founded E-Group as a fresh graduate in 1993 alongside András Nagy. Established E-Group Asia (Hong Kong) and E-Group Vietnam. As CEO, represents strategy, innovation, sales and business development. Advocates the 'data to knowledge with trust' vision. Coined #DoersParadise.
AN
András Nagy
Co-founder & CTO — Chief of Technology
M.Sc. Telecommunication Engineering and Computer Science, BME. IBM Watson Heidelberg. CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor). Personally designed and implemented KAÜ — Hungary's national digital authorisation service. Managed South East Asian State Digital Authorization Service. Leads engineering, product technology, and the delivery office. Deep expertise from applied cryptography to mission-critical infrastructure, from legacy systems to AI SmartData solutions. Co-founded E-Group as a fresh graduate in 1993.
