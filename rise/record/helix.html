What It Is

HeliX is a European health data space — a consortium of 50+ partner organisations sharing and analysing health data across institutional boundaries while maintaining full data sovereignty. No patient data leaves the institution that holds it. Instead, AI models travel to the data, train locally, and return aggregated insights.

E-Group's FedX platform is the federated learning infrastructure that makes this possible. FedX is the technical layer that allows HeliX partners to run federated queries, train shared models, and conduct cross-biobank analysis — without any institution needing to expose or transfer raw data.

Scale and Status

FedX is not a prototype or a pilot concept. As confirmed at the International Conference on Systems Biology (ICSB 2025), FedX is operationally validated — running in production across:

  • 8 biobanks across Europe
  • 50+ partner organisations in the HeliX consortium
  • Cross-institution federated queries running in production

A Clinical Geneticist at Semmelweis University — one of E-Group's HeliX partners — noted: for the first time, their institution could perform federated queries across multiple institutions without moving the data or compromising patient privacy.

Research Output

E-Group and BME jointly presented a research poster at ICSB 2025: "Cross-Biobank Analysis by Transformers." This work introduced MQTT — a novel transformer architecture creating unified representations from diverse patient data (genetic, laboratory, prescription) using federated learning across different biobanks.

This joint presentation, led by Dr. Péter Antal, exemplifies E-Group's approach: academic rigour combined with production-ready infrastructure.

General Assembly in Budapest

The HeliX consortium held its second in-person General Assembly (HGA) on 10–11 March 2026 in Budapest, Hungary — hosted at E-Group's facilities. As the primary decision-making body of the consortium, all partner organisations were represented. The HGA convenes every six months per the project's grant agreement.
