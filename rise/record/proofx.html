What It Is

ProofX is E-Group's cryptographic image verification system that creates tamper-proof evidence chains for digital imagery. Built in partnership with the European Space Agency, ProofX combines Galileo satellite authentication with C2PA content provenance standards to establish unbreakable proof of when, where, and by whom an image was captured.

The system addresses a fundamental challenge in the AI era: distinguishing between authentic photographs and AI-generated imagery. ProofX doesn't analyze images after capture — it creates cryptographic proof at the moment of capture that travels with the image permanently and cannot be forged.

ESA Partnership

ProofX is being developed through an active partnership with the European Space Agency, leveraging ESA's Galileo satellite constellation and OSNMA (Open Service Navigation Message Authentication) protocol. This partnership provides access to space-based cryptographic timestamps that form the foundation of ProofX's evidence chains.

The collaboration represents E-Group's position in the European space technology ecosystem, working directly with ESA on commercial applications of satellite authentication rather than simply licensing existing technologies. ProofX is among the first commercial products to implement Galileo OSNMA for image verification.

Technical Innovation

ProofX employs a multi-layered approach to cryptographic image verification, combining space-based authentication with industry-standard content provenance protocols. The system creates an immutable record that binds each image to verified location, time, and creator identity.

  • Galileo OSNMA authentication provides satellite-verified timestamps at capture
  • C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity) standards ensure tamper-proof metadata chains
  • eIDAS digital identity integration establishes verified creator identity
  • Court-admissible evidence format designed for legal proceedings
  • EU AI Act compliance for synthetic media transparency requirements
  • Real-time verification without dependence on external databases

Applications

ProofX targets sectors where photographic authenticity has legal, financial, or operational consequences. The system provides court-admissible evidence chains for industries that depend on verified imagery, eliminating disputes about image manipulation or synthetic content.

Primary applications include insurance claim documentation, media and journalism verification, facility management and compliance photography, and defense-adjacent reconnaissance where image authenticity is operationally critical. Each use case benefits from ProofX's ability to provide definitive proof of image origin and integrity.

The system is optimized for integration with professional imaging workflows, supporting both handheld devices and specialized equipment like the Airlot S1 drone for aerial intelligence applications requiring cryptographic verification.
