What We Built

Settlement systems.
Market modelling.
Regulatory readiness.

The E-Group systems running at MAVIR cover the full operational lifecycle of electricity market management — from real-time settlement to regulatory compliance to master data governance.

Settlement System Support
Mission-critical settlement infrastructure supporting KÁT, METÁR, balancing energy, and tiered pricing structures. 15-second and 1-minute settlement window capability — built for the speed that grid operations demand.
Market Modelling
Analytical systems for electricity market modelling, forecasting, and scenario analysis. Supporting MAVIR's decision-making on grid balancing, cross-border capacity, and renewable integration.
Regulatory Change Management
Continuous adaptation to EU energy market regulation changes — ENTSO-E requirements, national regulatory updates, and cross-border harmonisation. Built to evolve with the regulatory landscape.
Renewable Energy Market Support
Systems supporting Hungary's renewable energy market mechanisms including KÁT (mandatory off-take) and METÁR (premium support scheme). Built as the market transitioned from fossil to renewable generation.
Master Data Registry
Central master data management for market participants, metering points, and grid assets. The authoritative record for Hungary's electricity market — built and maintained by E-Group.
Customer Database Integration
Integration layer connecting MAVIR's operational systems with customer-facing services, billing infrastructure, and cross-border market interfaces.
