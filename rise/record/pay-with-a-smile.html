Multi-Layer Biometric Payment Solution

Pay with a Smile is E-Group's multi-layer biometric payment solution that uses face and palm recognition for seamless payment processes. The system provides secure, contactless payment automation for frequent purchases, eliminating the need for traditional payment cards or mobile devices during transactions.

Launched in September 2019, Pay with a Smile represents next-generation digital payment technology that combines convenience with enterprise-grade security. The solution was demonstrated at the EIT Digital Conference in Brussels 2019, showcasing European innovation in financial technology.

Biometric Authentication Technology

The system employs dual-layer biometric verification combining face recognition and palm recognition technologies. This multi-layer approach ensures both security and user convenience:

  • Face recognition for primary user identification
  • Palm recognition for secondary authentication
  • Real-time biometric processing and verification
  • Secure biometric data storage and encryption
  • Fast authentication suitable for retail environments

EIT Digital Partnership and Innovation

Pay with a Smile was developed as part of E-Group's EIT Digital Innovation partnership, representing collaborative European financial technology development. The solution was featured at the EIT Digital Conference in Brussels 2019, demonstrating cutting-edge biometric payment capabilities to European financial institutions and technology partners.

This partnership positioned E-Group at the forefront of European FinTech innovation, contributing to the development of next-generation payment technologies that prioritize both security and user experience.

Trust Infrastructure Product Line

Pay with a Smile is part of E-Group's Trust infrastructure product line, leveraging thirty years of experience in building secure, reliable systems for financial institutions. The biometric payment solution applies the same rigorous security standards and infrastructure expertise that powers E-Group's banking and government systems.

Key capabilities include secure payment processing, fraud prevention, real-time transaction monitoring, and compliance with European financial regulations and data protection standards.

Use Cases and Applications

The system is designed for frequent purchase payment automation in environments where speed and convenience are essential:

  • Retail point-of-sale systems
  • Corporate cafeterias and employee facilities
  • Transit systems and transportation hubs
  • Educational institution payment systems
  • Healthcare facility payment processing

Pay with a Smile eliminates the friction of traditional payment methods while maintaining the security standards required for financial transactions.
