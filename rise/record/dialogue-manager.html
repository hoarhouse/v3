AI System Combining Human and Artificial Assistants

The Self-Learning Dialogue Manager is an AI-powered customer service system that seamlessly combines human and artificial assistants to optimize customer communication. The system automatically handles customer communication tasks, identifies task types, and intelligently routes customers to human operators through call centers or chat interfaces when needed.

This major EU-funded project was completed on November 20, 2021, representing a significant achievement in hybrid AI-human collaboration for customer service automation.

EU Funding and Project Scale

The project received 238,160,500 HUF in total funding, with 64.08% support from the European Union through the VEKOP program (Project ID: VEKOP-2.1.1-15-2016-00195). This substantial investment reflects the strategic importance of developing advanced AI customer service capabilities within the European technology ecosystem.

The funding enabled E-Group to develop state-of-the-art dialogue management technology that bridges the gap between fully automated systems and human customer service representatives.

Hybrid AI Architecture

The system implements a hybrid approach to customer service, combining the efficiency of AI with the empathy and problem-solving capabilities of human agents:

  • Automatic task identification and classification
  • Self-learning capabilities that improve over time
  • Intelligent routing to appropriate human operators
  • Seamless handoff between AI and human assistants
  • Continuous learning from human agent interactions

Automation and Task Routing

The Dialogue Manager provides automatic task identification and customer routing capabilities that optimize customer service operations. The system analyzes incoming customer communications, identifies the nature of the request, and determines whether it can be handled automatically or requires human intervention.

This intelligent routing ensures customers receive appropriate assistance quickly while optimizing the use of human customer service resources for complex or sensitive issues.

Multi-Channel Integration

The system supports both call center and chat interface integration, providing consistent service across multiple communication channels:

  • Voice-based interactions through call center systems
  • Text-based communications via chat interfaces
  • Unified dialogue management across channels
  • Context preservation during channel switching
  • Consistent customer experience regardless of channel

Applications and Impact

The Self-Learning Dialogue Manager enables customer service automation, task routing, and communication management across various industries and use cases. The system's self-learning capabilities mean it continuously improves its performance, learning from both successful automated interactions and human agent interventions.

By combining the strengths of AI and human intelligence, the system delivers efficient, scalable customer service while maintaining the human touch when needed. This approach represents the future of customer service — where AI handles routine tasks while humans focus on complex, high-value interactions.
