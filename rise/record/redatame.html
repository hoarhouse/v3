What It Is

reData.me is a consent-based personal data sharing and monetisation platform built by E-Group. It operates as a three-party marketplace connecting Data Subjects (individuals), Data Users (companies that want data), and Data Holders (companies that hold data).

The platform has its own live website at redata.me, its own branding, and its own product identity. The tagline is "Responsible Reuse of Data."

How It Works

Data Subjects (individuals) control their own data. They manage consents, review offers from platform members, and decide exactly what data gets shared and with whom.

Data Users (companies buying data) access validated, consented data through a single platform integration. They can issue better product offers, build AI-driven solutions, and comply with regulatory requirements.

Data Holders (companies holding data) monetise their data assets by selling to Data Users through one connection to the reData.me platform.

reData.me acts as an encrypted intermediary. Data is end-to-end encrypted in transit — not even reData.me can read the data being transferred. Only the approved recipient can decrypt and access it.

Business Model

Pay-as-you-go. Integrate once, connect to many. The model incentivises transaction volume — creating an organically growing network for secure data sharing.

Compliance

reData.me monitors and complies with: GDPR, PSD2, eIDAS, the Data Governance Act, and the Digital Markets Act. SAML protocol and advanced encryption ensure maximum security of all data transmission.

Application Areas

The platform supports over ten distinct application areas:

  • Personal identification and data collection from official data sources
  • Digital contracting and e-signature
  • Health data sharing for personalised medicine and patient follow-up
  • Wearable data for insight-based insurance offers
  • Customer onboarding with validation
  • Credit history scores for product offers and client validation
  • Banking account history for customised investment offers
  • Location, parking, and traffic data for smart city design
  • Early detection signals for health and life insurance products
  • Remote medical monitoring with smart devices
  • Research on rare diseases using lifestyle data correlations
  • Early detection of neurological diseases in children

Deployed Case Studies

Insurance Sector: An insurance company uses reData.me for identification data, health history, and official medical records to automate low-volume claim disbursement and multi-party contract signing.

Telecommunication Sector: A telecom network provider gives special offers to clients in exchange for access to their banking history data, combined with e-ID reading services through the reData.me platform.
