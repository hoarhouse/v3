Who We Are

Not a consultancy.
Not a startup.
A technology group.

E-Group was founded in Budapest in 1993 with a single conviction: that the most important problems in digital systems are problems of trust, identity, and data integrity. Everything else — the features, the interfaces, the applications — is built on top of those foundations.

For thirty years we have been building those foundations. National digital identity infrastructure. Sovereign data platforms. Post-quantum cryptography. Payment clearing systems. Financial messaging networks. AI research that wins international competitions.

We build across FinTech, HealthTech, GovTech, SpaceTech, and EnergyTech. We have active EU programme partnerships at the highest level. And we are now building intelligence products that put thirty years of domain knowledge to work for any enterprise serious enough to use it.

Headquartered in Budapest with R&D offices in Pécs and Miskolc, and international offices in Hong Kong (E-Group Asia) and Vietnam. Our network spans Europe, the US, and Asia — 90+ professionals and 50+ external associates.

Hungary has a culture of rigorous, original thinking. Of building things that matter. Of taking hard problems seriously and working on them until they are solved — not until the funding runs out or the press release is written.

E-Group was founded in Budapest in 1993. We are still here. We are still building. We consider ourselves part of that tradition.

Thirty Years

Not a pitch.
A history.

1993
Founded in Budapest
E-Group established with a focus on financial software and digital trust infrastructure. The conviction from day one: the most important problems in computing are problems of trust and identity.
Origin
1999 — ongoing
MAVIR — National Electricity Grid
Partnership with Hungary's national electricity transmission operator begins. Over 25 years of mission-critical infrastructure delivery on settlement systems, market modelling, and regulatory change management. The longest client relationship in E-Group's history — and still active today.
25+ Year Partnership
2000s
Financial Infrastructure at Scale
Built clearing, settlement, and payment gateway infrastructure for Hungarian and Central European financial institutions. First large-scale proof that trust infrastructure at national scale is achievable.
2000s — ongoing
CORPEX — Corporate Banking Infrastructure
Corporate internet banking front-end and financial infrastructure platform. Built and deployed for financial institutions across Central Europe. Live in production — proven over two decades of financial operations.
Live in Production
2010s
KAÜ — Hungary's National Digital Identity
Designed and built Hungary's KAÜ national digital authorisation service — personally led by CTO András Nagy. The system authenticates millions of Hungarian citizens across all government services. The most significant trust infrastructure project in the country's digital history.
Landmark
2019
Post-Quantum Cryptography Research
E-Group begins active research into post-quantum cryptographic systems — six years before most enterprises begin treating this as an urgent problem. The infrastructure being built today will outlast current encryption standards.
2022
Gaia-X Hungary — Industry Representative
E-Group represented Hungarian industry at the launch of the Gaia-X National Hub in Hungary — the European sovereign cloud initiative. Antal Kuthy and Tamás Bihal attended alongside government officials and international Gaia-X leadership.
EU Recognition

Formal AI research group established under Dr. Péter Antal. The programme combines academic rigour with enterprise application — research that wins international competitions and ships production products.
