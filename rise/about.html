About E-Group

We started building AI infrastructure
before anyone called it that.

Founded in Budapest in 1993. Still here. Still building. Thirty years of trust infrastructure is not a marketing claim — it is a record. This page is that record.

Who We Are

Not a consultancy. Not a startup. A technology group with thirty years of deep IP.

E-Group was founded in Budapest in 1993 with a single conviction: that the most important problems in digital systems are problems of trust, identity, and data integrity. Everything else — the features, the interfaces, the applications — is built on top of those foundations.

For thirty years we have been building those foundations. National digital identity infrastructure. Sovereign data platforms. Post-quantum cryptography. Payment clearing systems. Financial messaging networks. AI research that wins international competitions.

We are 110 engineers, scientists, and builders. We work across FinTech, HealthTech, GovTech, SpaceTech, and EnergyTech. We have active EU project partnerships. And now we are packaging thirty years of domain knowledge into intelligence products that any enterprise can deploy.

"We have been building the infrastructure others will need for decades. The world has finally caught up. The question now is not whether AI needs trust infrastructure — it is who has spent the time building it properly."

Antal Kuthy — Founder & CEO, E-Group · Est. 1993
The Record

Thirty years.
Not a pitch. A history.

1993
Founded in Budapest

E-Group established with a focus on financial software and digital trust infrastructure. The conviction from day one: the most important problems in computing are problems of trust and identity.

Origin
2000s
Financial Infrastructure at Scale

Built clearing, settlement, and payment gateway infrastructure for Hungarian and Central European financial institutions. First large-scale proof that trust infrastructure at national scale is achievable.

2010s
National Digital Identity — KAÜ

Designed and built Hungary's KAÜ national digital identity infrastructure. The system that authenticates millions of Hungarian citizens across government services. The most significant trust infrastructure project in the country's digital history.

Landmark
2019
Post-Quantum Cryptography Research

E-Group begins active research into post-quantum cryptographic systems — six years before most enterprises begin treating this as an urgent problem. The infrastructure being built today will outlast current encryption standards.

2022
AI Research Programme Established

Formal AI research group established under Dr. Péter Antal, Associate Professor and Head of AI Research. The programme combines academic rigour with enterprise application — research that wins competitions and ships products.

2024
EU DOME Cloud Infrastructure

Selected as Hungary's representative for the EU DOME federated cloud initiative — the EU's sovereign cloud infrastructure project. Formal recognition at European level of E-Group's position as a trusted infrastructure partner.

EU Recognition
2024
Medical AI — Back-to-Back International First Place

E-Group's HealthTech AI research team takes first place at two consecutive international medical AI research events. Dr. Anna Kovács leads a team that is producing research that the global academic community is taking seriously.

Research Win
2025
Dr. Péter Antal — Knight's Cross of the Order of Merit

E-Group's lead AI researcher awarded Hungary's Knight's Cross of the Order of Merit on August 19, 2025 — the civilian division's highest recognition. A personal honour that reflects the quality of E-Group's research programme.

2025
Kellton Partnership — Sovereign AI Ecosystem MoU

Memorandum of Understanding signed with Kellton to build a sovereign AI ecosystem for European enterprise — one that does not depend on US hyperscalers. The infrastructure layer for European AI independence.

Now
Rise + Intelligence Products

50 engineers building Rise — the dual-consciousness AI platform — from spec. Intelligence Products in active development across FinTech, HealthTech, GovTech, SpaceTech, and EnergyTech. Packaging thirty years for any enterprise to deploy.
