## Live in Production

### FedX
Federated learning platform for health data. Data stays local — AI travels across it. Operationally validated across 8 biobank clients and 50+ healthcare providers in the HeliX Health Data Space. EHDEN certified. GDPR compliant. One of Europe's largest sovereign health data ecosystems.
**Status:** Live in production
**Sector:** HealthTech
**Record page:** record/helix.html

### KAÜ — National Digital Identity
Hungary's national digital authorisation service. Designed and implemented personally by Co-founder András Nagy. Authenticates millions of Hungarian citizens across all government services. The most significant trust infrastructure project in Hungary's digital history.
**Status:** Live in production — operational since 2010s
**Sector:** GovTech
**Record page:** record/kau.html

### CORPEX
Correspondent banking infrastructure. Live in production for financial institutions. Built on E-Group's thirty years of financial trust infrastructure — clearing, settlement, and payment systems proven at national scale.
**Status:** Live in production
**Sector:** FinTech
**Record page:** [TIBOR TO CONFIRM — page to be created]

### MyD Wallet
E-Group's eIDAS 2.0 digital identity wallet. Active member of the European Digital Identity Wallet Consortium (EWC). Passed all eIDAS 2 testbed scenarios. Backward compatible with KAÜ, forward compatible with the EU-wide 2026 rollout.
**Status:** Live / testbed passed
**Sector:** GovTech / Identity
**Record page:** record/eidas.html
