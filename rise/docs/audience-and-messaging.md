## Audience 1 — Existing Customers

### Who they are
Companies and institutions that already have a working relationship with E-Group. They have seen E-Group deliver. They trust the team.

Known examples: MAVIR, Hungarian Government, [TIBOR TO CONFIRM — full client list]

### What they already know
- E-Group delivers what it promises
- The team is serious and technically capable
- E-Group has been around long enough to not be a risk

### What they don't yet know
- The full breadth of what E-Group builds across other sectors
- What E-Group is building next
- That E-Group has EU-level recognition and partnerships

### What we want them to feel
Proud to be associated with E-Group. Curious about what else is being built. Confident referring E-Group to their peers.

### What we want them to do
Return to the site regularly. Share specific pages with colleagues. Expand their relationship with E-Group into new areas.

### Key messages for this audience
- "You already know what we can do. Here's what else we're building."
- "The same rigour that delivered your project is now being applied to these new areas."
- "Your peers in other sectors are starting to notice us."

## Audience 2 — The Warm Circle

### Who they are
Companies and institutions that know of E-Group through consortium memberships, EU initiatives, trade groups, and industry events. They have seen E-Group at the table. They have not yet worked directly with them.

Examples: IPCEI-CIS partners (CloudFerro, Engineering Ingegneria, Orange, IONOS, Infobip, Reply, Tiscali), HeliX consortium members, DOME participants, EWC members, 4iG, Hungarian tech ecosystem contacts.

### What they already know
- E-Group exists and is active in European tech infrastructure
- E-Group participates in serious EU programmes
- E-Group is Hungarian, technical, and has been around a long time

### What they don't yet know
- The depth and scale of what E-Group has actually built
- That E-Group has 25+ year client relationships with critical infrastructure operators
- That E-Group has ESA partnerships and internationally award-winning AI research
- That E-Group is building products they might actually want to use or partner on

### What we want them to feel
Surprised by the depth of E-Group's track record. Curious about the new products. Confident that E-Group is a serious partner worth a conversation.

### What we want them to do
Read at least two pages. Follow E-Group on LinkedIn. Reach out about a specific product or partnership. Refer E-Group to a colleague with a relevant problem.

### Key messages for this audience
- "You've seen us in the room. Here's what we've actually built."
- "We've been solving the problem you're working on for longer than you might think."
- "We're not pitching — we're building. Come see what we're working on."

## The Conversation We're Trying to Start

Not a sales funnel. Not a lead generation machine. A trust-building platform that makes the first real conversation easier.

The ideal outcome: a warm circle contact takes a call with Antal or Tibor having already read the MAVIR credential, the ESA partnership, and one Thinking article. That person is not a cold prospect. They are already halfway convinced.

---

## What Never Works on This Audience

- Generic AI claims ("we harness the power of AI to transform your business")
- Vague future promises without present proof
- Anything that sounds like it was written by a marketing department
- Tech bro energy of any kind
