SpaceTech · Authentication
ProofX
Cryptographic image and video verification. Combines Galileo OSNMA satellite authentication with C2PA tamper-proof media standards and eIDAS digital identity. The result: court-admissible evidence chains for photographs and video. Built for insurance claim submission, media authentication, facility management, and defence-adjacent applications.
Why it matters — AI-generated imagery is now indistinguishable to the human eye. ProofX answers the question that matters: was this taken where and when it claims to be taken?
ESA Partnership Active
SpaceTech · Geospatial
GENESIS
Synthetic satellite imagery generation and analysis. Built for organisations operating in space data, defence-adjacent applications, and geospatial intelligence. Developed in active partnership with the European Space Agency.
Why it matters — The gap between what satellite data can tell you and what organisations can currently extract from it is enormous. SpadaX closes that gap.

FinTech · AI Infrastructure
COIOS
AI operating system for financial institutions. Built on E-Group's thirty years of FinTech infrastructure — clearing, settlement, RegTech, risk intelligence, PSD2 and DORA compliance.
