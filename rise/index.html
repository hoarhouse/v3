Who We Are

The infrastructure
beneath the intelligence.

Founded in Budapest in 1993, E-Group has spent thirty years solving the hard problems that make AI, data and digital trust actually work at scale. We are not a consultancy. We are not a startup. We are a technology group with deep IP, a world-class engineering team, and a singular focus: building the infrastructure that enterprises need to operate in an AI-driven world.

We have built national digital identity systems, sovereign data platforms, post-quantum encryption infrastructure, and AI tools that are used at the highest levels of European government. Now we are packaging that expertise into products that any enterprise can deploy.
