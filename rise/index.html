Hungary is a country of 10 million people that has produced 13 Nobel laureates, invented modern computing, holography, and the mRNA vaccine.

John von Neumann designed the architecture every computer still runs on. Katalin Karikó's mRNA research saved millions of lives. Dénes Gábor invented holography. These are not coincidences — Hungary has a culture of rigorous, original thinking. Of building things that last.

E-Group was founded here in 1993. Still here. Still building. We consider that a point of pride.
