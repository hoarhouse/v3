The problem

Most parishes are
invisible online.

There are over 221,000 Catholic parishes in the world. The vast majority have no website, no way to accept donations online, and no connection to the global Church community. They serve faithfully — but quietly, alone, and without the tools they deserve.

Communio changes all of that. For free.

How it works

Up and running
in days, not months.

Step 01
Register in under 10 minutes
We look up your parish automatically and pre-fill what we know. You just confirm the details, describe your community, and optionally connect your bank account for donations.
~10 minutes
Step 02
DCF reviews your application
Every parish is personally reviewed by the DCF team. This protects the network's integrity. You'll get an email decision with your login credentials.
3–5 business days
Step 03
Your parish goes live
Profile page published on the global map. Donation page active. Dashboard access granted — build your website, post events, message other parishes.
Instant on approval
Step 04
Grow with the network
As more parishes join, the community grows stronger. Participate in global campaigns, form sister-parish relationships, and amplify your collective voice on the issues that matter.
