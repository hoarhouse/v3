Institutions &
Partners

Nine of the world's most respected institutions in science, medicine, peace, and faith — united by a shared commitment to humanity's future.

Nobel Laureates Assembly for the Prevention of Nuclear War
Intellectual Leadership
Provides the moral authority, global visibility, and scientific credibility at the heart of the Assembly.
University of Chicago · Existential Risk Laboratory
Academic Partner
Contributes academic expertise and analytical capacity in existential risk, AI governance, and nuclear policy.
International Physicians for the Prevention of Nuclear War (IPPNW)
Scientific & Medical Partner
Provides scientific, medical, and policy analysis on the humanitarian consequences of nuclear conflict and pathways toward risk reduction.
University of Notre Dame · Kroc Institute
Ethics & Policy Partner
Contributes ethical, legal, and policy perspectives, including insights grounded in Catholic social teaching and international peacebuilding.
Soka Gakkai International
Peace Education Partner
Contributes global experience in peace education, cultural transformation, and public engagement in support of nuclear disarmament.
Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists
Scientific Communication
Home of the Doomsday Clock — a trusted voice on nuclear risk and the dangers of emerging technologies since 1945.
Harvard Medical School · Brigham and Women's Hospital
Medical & Scientific Partner
Contributes medical and public health expertise on the catastrophic humanitarian consequences of nuclear weapons use.
Pugwash Conferences on Science and World Affairs
Policy & Disarmament Partner
The Nobel Peace Prize-winning organization founded on the Russell-Einstein Manifesto — a direct intellectual ancestor of this Assembly.
Domus Communis Foundation
General Secretariat
Serves as General Secretariat of the Assembly, ensuring strategic coordination, institutional relations, and administrative management in close connection with the Borgo Laudato Si'.
Global Nobel Laureates Assembly on Artificial Intelligence and Nuclear War
Borgo Laudato Si' · Castel Gandolfo, Vatican
14–16 July 2026
