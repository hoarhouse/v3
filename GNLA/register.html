Attend · Follow · Engage

Be part of
this moment

Whether you join us at the Vatican, follow proceedings online, or carry the Rome Declaration forward in your work — this Assembly belongs to everyone who believes humanity can choose a different future.

In-Person Attendance

Attendance at the Assembly is by invitation and subject to review by the Organizing Committee. We welcome requests from individuals whose expertise, institutional affiliation, or public role is directly relevant to the Assembly's mandate.

Requests will be reviewed on a rolling basis. Applicants will be notified by 30 June 2026.

What to expect
  • →Access to open sessions and public panels
  • →Attendance at the Rome Declaration press conference
  • →Networking with global leaders, scientists, and policymakers
  • →Official Assembly materials and documentation

Your request will be reviewed by the Organizing Committee. We will respond within 10 business days.

Press Accreditation

The Assembly welcomes accredited journalists, photographers, and broadcast media. Press access is available to open sessions and the global press conference on 16 July 2026.

Press credentials must be submitted by 30 June 2026. Access to the press kit and embargoed materials will be provided upon accreditation.
