Why This Assembly

A convergence of risks
unprecedented in scale

The integration of artificial intelligence into military infrastructure — including command, control, and decision-support systems — introduces dynamics of speed, opacity, and automation that may fundamentally alter how existential decisions are made.

At the same time, the global geopolitical landscape is entering a renewed phase of fragmentation, marked by the re-emergence of nuclear deterrence doctrines and a gradual erosion of arms control frameworks.

Read the Full Brief
The Risk
AI & Nuclear Convergence
Automated systems operating at machine speed may compress the time available for human judgment in high-stakes strategic decisions.
The Stakes
Civilizational Consequence
Decisions of existential consequence may become increasingly mediated or influenced by algorithmic systems — without adequate governance frameworks.
The Response
An Unarmed & Disarming Peace
This Assembly challenges the assumption that security must be grounded in deterrence — and charts a different path toward structural, lasting peace.
The Output
The Rome Declaration
A founding text for a renewed global security framework, to be signed and announced publicly on 16 July 2026.

Venue

Borgo Laudato Si'
Castel Gandolfo

Set within the Papal Gardens overlooking Lake Albano, Borgo Laudato Si' is a setting of rare spiritual and institutional authority — a place commensurate with the weight of this moment.

Located just 25 kilometres from Rome, the venue provides both the serenity required for genuine deliberation and the proximity to global media and institutional infrastructure.
