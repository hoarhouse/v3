01

Compressed Decision Timelines

AI-driven systems shrink the time available for human judgment in nuclear scenarios — turning minutes into seconds, elevating the risk of irreversible miscalculation.

02

Eroding Arms Control

The collapse of multilateral agreements and re-emergence of deterrence doctrines leave critical gaps in the architecture that has kept nuclear peace since 1945.

03

A Governance Vacuum

Autonomous weapons and AI integration have advanced without corresponding international norms — a vacuum that grows more dangerous with every passing month.
